About us

A word about us

JoLikod has been serving delicious meals to the Filipino community for over 18 years.

Mission: To bring joy and flavor to every table through our carefully crafted dishes.

Vision: To be the preferred choice for authentic, fresh, and flavorful meals in the Philippines.

Motto: Good food, great smiles.

Our team of passionate developers and chefs have built this legacy to combine technology, creativity, and culinary excellence for the benefit of every customer.
